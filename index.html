About Diana

I specialize in teaching piano and am especially interested in discovering parallels between music-making and other lifestyle activities.

I strive to highlight my students' existing strengths and channel those qualities into their music. My role is to guide my students in developing into the best version of themselves. Whether it is through group or private piano lessons, precision, perserverance, authenticity of expression, and vulnerability are the key ingredients to success. No different than the busy daily lives of modern people, we celebrate successes, experience setbacks, and incrementally become better than we were yesterday. I invite you to channel your musical experience into your everyday life and integrate the benefits of music into your personal goals.
